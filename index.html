Hi! I believe that while healing can be done alone, peer support is super helpful and in some cases, necessary. I genuinely believe the community aspect of healing is oft overlooked.

This is a community-first space; by the community for the community. We share resources, stories, experiences.
Because your voice matters. Because we can heal as a community. Share everything you've found helpful in your journey and take a look at everything that has helped others.
